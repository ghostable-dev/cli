# Security Policy

Ghostable is built upon a zero-knowledge, device-scoped cryptographic architecture, ensuring strong protection and privacy of your data.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you believe you have identified a security vulnerability, **please do not open a public GitHub issue**. Instead, responsibly disclose the details by emailing [security@ghostable.dev](mailto:security@ghostable.dev). We commit to acknowledging your report within **24 hours** and working closely with you to investigate and address the vulnerability as quickly as possible.

Thank you for your assistance in keeping Ghostable secure for all users.
